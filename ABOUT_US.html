Tips And Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make your experience more smooth-sailing!

Time Your Moves

Carefully watch the block move back and forth a few times so that you can get an idea of its speed.

Keep Finger Close To Mouse

To minimize tapping delays, try to hover your finger close to the screen.



Focus On One Corner

It's easier to focus on only one point of the block.

Click/Tap Lightly

The game might consider a heavy click as two clicks which may cause your block to fall earlier than wanted.

Accuracy Matters

A bigger base allows for more room for error.
